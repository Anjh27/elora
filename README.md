# Elora

Elora is a fashion accessories brand founded by **Jap Ajooni Kour** and **Mannat Kohli**.

We specialize in stylish and affordable fashion accessories designed to help people express their individuality and confidence.

## About Us

Elora was created with a passion for fashion, creativity, and self-expression. Our goal is to provide trendy accessories that complement every style and occasion.
